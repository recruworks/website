How it works

Streamlined Recruitment at Your Fingertips

1

Sign Up

Joining Recruworks is quick and easy. Employers and candidates can register by providing essential details to create their profiles.
2

 Post Jobs or Apply

Employers can post job opportunities with detailed descriptions and requirements. Candidates can explore job listings and apply seamlessly through the platform.
3

AI Matching

Our cutting-edge AI technology goes to work, matching the right candidates with the most suitable job opportunities. Employers receive curated candidate suggestions, while candidates get notified about relevant job matches.
4

Communication Hub

Recruworks serves as a centralized communication hub. Employers and candidates can interact, schedule interviews, and exchange information – all within the platform.
5

Assessment and Evaluation

Employers can conduct assessments and evaluations directly on the platform. From skills tests to video interviews, streamline your candidate selection process.
6

Connect with Ease

Found your ideal match? Connect effortlessly through the platform. Employers can extend offers, and candidates can accept or negotiate terms, making the entire process smooth and transparent.
7

Track Progress

Stay informed about your recruitment journey. Employers can track candidate progress, and candidates can monitor the status of their applications, ensuring transparency and accountability.
